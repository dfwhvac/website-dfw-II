## 🔎 SERP Character Budget Enforcement Rules

1. **Hard max:** 60 characters (Google truncates mobile SERP between 55–65; desktop 60).
2. **Brand always last**, separated by `|` (not `-` or `::`).
3. **Keyword-first, geo-second, modifier-third, brand-last.**
4. **Review-count insertion:** only use when title has ≥10 chars of headroom after keyword + geo + brand.
5. **Star character:** `★` (U+2605) — renders in Google SERP as black star. Do not use emoji ⭐ (often stripped by Google).
6. **No ALL CAPS. No exclamation.** (Google downweights.)
7. **No year in evergreen titles.** ("2026" invites future-year rot.)
