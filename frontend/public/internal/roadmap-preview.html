Critical Sequencing Rules

  1. GBP verification (P1.8) starts on Day 1 of Phase 2b. Verification takes 5–14 days, blocks the 60-day GBP uplift compounding window. Do not delay.
  2. Microsoft Clarity (C1) installs on Day 1 of Phase 3. Needs 30+ days runtime to produce useful heatmaps before P1.10/P1.9 changes ship.
  3. CallRail/DNI (P2.19) must be live before Day 1 of Phase 5. Without it, you'll optimize ads on 20–40% of conversion signal.
  4. Phase 2b runs PARALLEL with Phase 3. Don't wait for the 28-week city content sprint to finish before shipping conversion fixes.
  5. No two phases skip ahead. Phase 1 must complete (or have ticketed plan) before Phase 2a starts. Phase 4 must NOT start before Phase 3 baseline is trending positive.
