6Strategic Notes

  1. /system-replacement is the revenue center. Everything points here: service pages cross-link, estimator funnel, financing support. Highest-ticket conversion surface on the site.
  2. /repair-or-replace is an AEO ambush. Decision-framework content with Article + FAQPage schema — exactly what AI Overviews cite. Low direct conversion; catches top-of-funnel replacement shoppers and routes them downward.
  3. /replacement-estimator solves the "not ready to publish prices" constraint. Users get a range without you committing to a fixed price. Every completion = a qualified lead. WebApplication + HowTo schema (S2, Apr 27) makes it eligible for AI-engine citation when answering "how to estimate hvac replacement cost dallas" queries.
  4. /financing is reachable in one click from every replacement surface. Replacement conversion depends on it.
  5. commercial-heating differentiation (A4, Apr 27) closes the only page Google explicitly rejected. 12 fields populated in Sanity + 6 commercial-only FAQs — RTU, boiler, VAV, NTE, COIs vocabulary now distinguishes it from the residential/heating sibling.
  6. AI crawlers (S1, Apr 27) are explicitly allowed. 15 named UAs across OpenAI, Anthropic, Perplexity, Google AI, Apple Intelligence, Common Crawl, Meta. Most agencies block these by default; we opt in for top-of-funnel visibility.
  7. Stubs aren't wasted work. URL structure reserved now prevents painful migrations later. noindex until ready.
  8. City-level replacement pages are Phase 2. Build 1 prototype, measure 28-day GSC demand, then scale — don't pre-build 28 pages without data.
  9. Sitemap freshness signal is honest. Policy pages pinned to POLICY_LASTMOD; Sanity-driven pages use _updatedAt; static-but-maintained pages stamp at build (S7, Apr 27).
