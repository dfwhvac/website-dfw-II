5Strategic Notes

  1. /system-replacement is the new revenue center. Everything points here: service pages cross-link, estimator funnel, financing support. Highest-ticket conversion surface on the site.
  2. /repair-or-replace is an AEO ambush. Decision-framework content — exactly what AI Overviews cite. Low direct conversion; catches top-of-funnel replacement shoppers and routes them downward.
  3. /estimator solves the "not ready to publish prices" constraint. Users get a range without you committing to a fixed price. Every completion = a qualified lead.
  4. /financing promoted from backlog. Replacement conversion depends on it — no point building the replacement page if financing isn't reachable in one click.
  5. Stubs aren't wasted work. URL structure reserved now prevents painful migrations later. noindex until ready.
  6. Top-line nav grows by only 1 item (+ "Estimator"). System Replacement lives inside the Residential dropdown + as a hero on /services. Financing + repair-or-replace live in footer / inline.
  7. City-level replacement pages are Phase 2. Build 1 prototype, measure 28-day GSC demand, then scale — don't pre-build 28 pages without data.
